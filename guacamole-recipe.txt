Ingredients:
 - 3 avocados
 - 2 cloves garlic
 - 1 roma tomato
 - 1 onion
 - 1 jalapeno pepper
 - 1 lime
 - cilantro
 - cumin
 - salt & pepper to taste

Directions:
 1. Dice avocado and place in medium bowl. Squeeze over with lime juice and mash, leaving it as chunky as you like.

 2. Dice tomato and onion. Mince garlic and jalapeno. Rough chop cilantro. Add all ingredients to bowl and mix.

 3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

 4. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in fridge until ready to serve.
